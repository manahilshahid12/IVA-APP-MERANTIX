# Tyson Foods — Company Overview

## About
Tyson Foods is one of the world's largest food companies and a recognized leader in protein.
Headquartered in Springdale, Arkansas, USA. Founded in 1935.
Produces beef, pork, chicken, and prepared foods under brands including Tyson, Jimmy Dean,
Hillshire Farm, Ball Park, Wright, Aidells, and State Fair.

## Core Business
- Chicken, beef, and pork processing and distribution
- Prepared and value-added food products
- International sales and export operations

## Competitive Advantage
- Scale: one of the largest protein producers globally
- Diversified protein portfolio across chicken, beef, pork
- Strong retail and foodservice distribution relationships
- Vertically integrated supply chain
