# Mensch und Maschine — Company Overview

## About
Mensch und Maschine (MuM) is a leading European provider of CAD/CAM/PDM software solutions,
headquartered in Wessling, Germany. Founded in 1984, the company distributes and develops
software for product design, engineering, and data management.

## Core Products & Services
- Autodesk software reselling and value-added services
- Proprietary MuM software products (CAD/CAM/PDM)
- Training, consulting, and support services
- Cloud and SaaS transition services for engineering teams

## Competitive Advantage
- Deep technical expertise spanning 40+ years in engineering software
- Strong recurring revenue base from software subscriptions and maintenance
- Dual business model: reseller margins + high-margin proprietary software
- Pan-European presence across 20+ countries
